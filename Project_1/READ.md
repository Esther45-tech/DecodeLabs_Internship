E-Commerce Sales Data Analysis. Project Overview: This project explores an e-commerce sales dataset to uncover meaningful insights into customer purchasing behavior, order performance and revenue trends. The dataset was cleaned and prepared for analysis to support data-driven business decision-making.
Business Problem. E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of transactional data every day. Without proper ana;ysis, it becomes difficult to identify sales trends, customer preferences, payment patterns and operational challenges. This project aims to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights.
